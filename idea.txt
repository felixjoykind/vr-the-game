- Player is a boy in future that has a VR glasses with which he can place new blocks on a level, but cannot remove them. 
- Also these blocks can only be seen when glasses are on.
- Glasses have limited blocks to place (depends on a level)
- Glasses can only be worn for limited time
- Player finishes a level when reachs portal to the next one
- Game ends when player finishes al the levels, so he goes back to his time (in future)
